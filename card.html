Bengal Tiger

known for their striking coat pattern of orange to reddish-orange fur with dark brown to black stripes. This pattern provides effective camouflage in their natural habitat.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Length: 9 to 10 feet (2.7 to 3 meters)
  • Lifespan: Approximately 10 to 15 years.
  • Habitat: Mangrove swamps, grasslands, and tropical rainforests

Bengal tigers are solitary animals and are known for their exceptional swimming ability. They often cool off in water bodies and are quite comfortable in water. As carnivores, their diet primarily consists of large ungulates such as deer, wild boar, and buffalo.
